Pumpkin Waffles

Ingredients
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup pumpkin
  • ⅔ cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp pumkin pie spice
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • ⅔ cup buttermilk
