Tacos with Guacamole

Ingredients
Meat Crumbles
  • 1 lb ground turkey (85-93% lean)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ⅓ cup onion, chopped)
  • 1-2 garlic cloves, pressed
  • ¼ tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp Sambal Oelek or Chili Garlic Sauce
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1-2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp mesquite seasoning (optional)
Guacamole
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon or lime juice
  • 2 tbsp Pace picante or other salsa
  • ¼ tsp garlic salt
  • 1 or 2 fresh tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro, diced
Instructions

In a large sauce pan, saute onion in olive oil over low heat until translucent. Mix dry spices in mortar or small bowl. Add meat to pan and cook until browned, flipping and chopping to make crumbles. Add dry spices and mix thoroughly to coat meat crumbles. Add garlic and Sambal Oelek or Chili Garlic Sauce, turn off stove heat and mix thoroughly as the pan cools down.
