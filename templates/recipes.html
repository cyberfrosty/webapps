Pumpkin Waffles

Ingredients
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup pumpkin
  • ⅔ cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp pumkin pie spice
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • ⅔ cup buttermilk
Instructions

Combine flour, oats, baking powder and spice in mixing bowl, push to side. Add egg on edge, beat by itself, add oil and pumpkin, mix to blend, add half of the buttermilk and blend. Mix together with flour mixture and remaining buttermilk to proper consistancy. Do not overmix.
