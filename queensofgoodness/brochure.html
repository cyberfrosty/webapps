Our Mission
Education & Informed Choices

At Queens of Goodness, we aim to make a positive impact in everything we do. Community outreach programs, educational events at local schools, teaching classes and collaboration with beekeepers and other local food enthusiasts is part of who we are. From reducing the consumption of refined sugars to raising awareness about the importance of supporting local beekeepers, we want to empower our customers to make healthier, more informed choices. We believe in the power of food to nourish not just the body, but the soul, and our products reflect that belief in every jar.

Honey Information

From the hive to the jar the honey never reaches 104°F (40°C)

The honey is never filtered, only coarsely strained to remove the larger pieces of wax


Storage Information

Store at room temperature 68-78°F (20-25°C) for the best consistency

Prolonged exposure to temperatures over 95°F (35°C) may result in some decrystallization, but the product is still good

Shelf life is 3 years from production date
