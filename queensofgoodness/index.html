Creamed Pure Wyoming Honey

Our honey is harvested from hives in Wyoming, a state known for its clean air and abundant wildflowers. We are beekeepers and work closely with local beekeepers who share our commitment to sustainable, ethical practices. Each batch is hand-crafted using a process that yields a velvety-smooth product while preserving all the natural antioxidants, enzymes, and nutritional benefits.

Whole Natural Ingredients

We carefully select spices and fruits from suppliers that share our commitment to bring you the highest quality pure natural foods. We use organic, single-origin, and fair-trade ingredients wherever we can. There are no extracts or refined ingredients in any of our blends. Just the flavor and nutrition from the honey, spices and whole fruits.
