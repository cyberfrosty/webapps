Raw Local Honey

Premium Creamed Raw Honey

Our premium creamed raw honey is crafted from raw honey sourced directly from trusted local beekeepers and enriched with fair-trade spices and nutrient-rich freeze-dried fruits. Each batch is hand-crafted using a proprietary week-long crystallization and blending process that yields a velvety-smooth, spreadable product with distinctive flavors and all the natural benefits of raw local honey.
