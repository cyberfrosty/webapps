Our Honey
Raw Local Honey

We are beekeepers and all of our honey is harvested from hives in Wyoming, a state known for its clean air and abundant wildflowers. We work closely with local beekeepers who share our commitment to sustainable, ethical practices. Each jar of creamed raw honey is carefully hand-crafted to preserve its natural enzymes, antioxidants, and health benefits, ensuring that you receive a product that is both delicious and good for you.

Our Mission
Education & Informed Choices

At Queens of Goodness, we aim to make a positive impact in everything we do. From reducing the consumption of refined sugars to raising awareness about the importance of supporting local beekeepers, we want to empower our customers to make healthier, more informed choices. We believe in the power of food to nourish not just the body, but the soul, and our products reflect that belief in every jar.

Our Story
Beekeeping

I got my first beehive in 2018 and in spite of all the rookie mistakes, had a successful first year and I was hooked. Subsequent years presented many learning experiences, which with help from mentors and the internet I was somehow able to keep my bees thriving and expanding. Just being a successful hobbyist with 5 hives is a lot of work and from the side-liner and commercial beekeepers I met I knew that was not the path for me. After retiring from a career as a NASA researcher, I needed to keep busy and decided to create business centered around bees and health. Healthy bees and other pollinators in our ecosystems, and healthy products for our customers and community. Our premium creamed raw honey products reflect our values and the vision to create something special for anyone to enjoy, knowing that we've preserved all the goodness from the bees and enhanced by creaming along with a few carefully chosen natural ingredients. -- Susan
